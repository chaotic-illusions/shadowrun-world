Data Bombs
Target: Individual slave devices in this selector. File-level bombs are assigned in each Paydata row.
Trigger: Successfully accessing file/device without defusing the data bomb. Deals (Rating)M damage to the Persona.
Detection: Successful Analyze Icon against protected file/device.
Removal: Successful subsystem test (Files or Slave).
Scramble IC
Target: Access/Slave elements and subsystem-wide Files here. File-level Scramble is assigned in each Paydata row.
Trigger (Exploding): Successful removal attempt without defusing. Rating(M) damage to the Persona.
Trigger (Poison): IC detects Decker. Repeats data destruction attempt until decrypted or crashed.
Detection: Successful Analyze Icon against protected item.
Removal: Sucessful decrypt or attack against the IC.
Worm IC
Target: Any subsystem.
Trigger: Any system test against the infected subsystem.
Detection: Successful Analyze Subsystem test.
Removal: Successful disinfect or attack against the IC.
